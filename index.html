SNAKE LAKE






ORIGINAL

This is the original version of snake. You control a snake and have to eat food to grow. Grow as big as you can but remember to not hit yourself or the borders as you will lose

POISON

It is the same as the original snake with an added twist. Sometimes a posion pellet will appear which looks just like the food. You will lose if you eat it.

OUR VERSION

This is our version of snake. You have to reach the food and eat it without hitting any of the walls. If you hit the walls you have to restart
